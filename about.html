The Challenge We intercept

Water-borne epidemics remain a critical challenge for community health security across developing municipal ecosystems. Traditional medical strategies rely on reactive containment frameworks—acting only after patients arrive at localized treatment nodes.

JalSuraksha AI flips the defensive paradigm. Developed as a cutting-edge technical solution for the Smart India Hackathon, our system serves as an early-stage warning grid that merges IoT telemetry with AI engines to prevent outbreaks before they start.

Our Vision

Zero-outbreak communities via predictive engineering.

Our Mission

Empower local administrations with automated diagnostic data.

Core Infrastructure

The Three Pillars of the Platform

IoT Telemetry Node

Continuous edge-computing sensor units measuring physical properties: pH fluctuations, high turbidity coefficients, complete dissolved solids (TDS), and residual free chlorine values.

Neural Predictive Engine

Ensemble machine learning algorithms trained on historical regional data, weather variables, and patient ingestion logs to project out specific epidemiological trends 14 days in advance.

Automated Alert Loop

Enterprise-grade alert protocols that automatically push notifications to municipal water boards, medical clinics, and local citizen phones when indicators cross critical parameters.

National Mission Alignment

JalSuraksha AI is architected to align with India's primary health and resource directives. The system maps operational analytics directly onto data schemas approved by the National Health Mission (NHM) and the Jal Jeevan Mission, supporting efficient government resource allocation.
