Smart Community Health Monitoring and Early Warning System for Water-Borne Diseases in Rural Northeast India

Project Overview

This project is designed to monitor water quality and detect possible outbreaks of water-borne diseases in rural communities. The system helps healthcare workers collect health data, monitor disease cases, and generate early warnings to reduce the spread of infections.
